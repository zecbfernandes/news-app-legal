2. Data We Collect

2.1 Account Data

  • Email address
  • Display name
  • Profile photo URL (if you sign in with Google)

2.2 Usage Data

  • Your native language and learning language preferences
  • App language preference
  • Articles you have read (reading history)
  • Daily reading streak count
  • Energy level
  • Number of articles read

2.3 Social Data

  • The list of users you follow and who follow you
  • Your social feed (articles read by people you follow)
  • Your discoverability preference (whether you appear in search results)

2.4 Technical Data

  • Firebase Cloud Messaging token (stored on your device and in our database)
  • Subscription plan status (free or premium)

2.5 Data We Do NOT Collect

  • We do not collect precise location data
  • We do not collect payment card details (handled entirely by Google Play Billing)
  • We do not sell your data to third parties

We retain your personal data for as long as your account is active. When you delete your account (via Settings → Account → Delete Account), all your data stored in our database is permanently deleted within 30 days.

Anonymous, aggregated data that cannot identify you may be retained for analytical purposes.

6. Your Rights Under GDPR

  • Right of access — request a copy of your personal data
  • Right to rectification — correct inaccurate data
  • Right to erasure — request deletion of your data ("right to be forgotten")
  • Right to restriction — request that we limit processing of your data
  • Right to data portability — receive your data in a machine-readable format
  • Right to object — object to processing based on legitimate interests
  • Right to withdraw consent — for processing based on consent, you may withdraw at any time via device settings

To exercise these rights, contact us at privacy@newsapp.pt. We will respond within 30 days.

You also have the right to lodge a complaint with the Portuguese data protection authority (CNPD) at www.cnpd.pt.

7. Data Security

  • Encryption in transit (HTTPS/TLS)
  • Firebase Security Rules restricting access to your own data
  • No client-side write access to other users' documents

No method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security.

8. Children's Privacy

The App is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us with personal data, please contact us and we will delete it promptly.
